Tourist Attractions in Ghazni

Explore the historical and cultural sites of Ghazni province

Minaret of Ghazni

Built in the 12th century, this minaret is one of Ghazni’s most famous historical landmarks.

Ghazni Citadel

An ancient fortress that offers a glimpse into the rich history of the region and stunning city views.

Tomb of Sultan Mahmud

The resting place of the famous Ghaznavid ruler, an important cultural heritage site.
