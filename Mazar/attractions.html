Tomb of Rabia Balkhi

The Tomb of Rabia Balkhi (also called the Shrine of Rabia Balkhi) is a cherished historical and cultural site located in the city of Balkh, near Mazar-e-Sharif in northern Afghanistan. It is dedicated to Rabia Balkhi, one of the earliest and most celebrated female poets of the Persian language and a powerful symbol of love, courage, and artistic expression.
