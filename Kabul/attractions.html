Famous Places in Kabul

Kabul Museum

The Kabul Museum preserves thousands of artifacts representing ancient civilizations, including Kushan, Greco-Bactrian, and Islamic periods.

It plays a vital role in protecting heritage and educating students and researchers about Afghanistan’s history.

Darul Aman Palace

Darul Aman Palace was built in early 20th century as part of modernization. European and Afghan architectural elements are combined.

It stands as a national symbol representing political and cultural transformation.
